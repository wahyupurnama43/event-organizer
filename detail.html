Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Resort, Indonesia

October 13, 2018

Description

Referred by Sofitel Bali, KITASATUBALI worked with the Secretary’s Department of the IMF to design and produce the meeting room for IMFC session during the IMF-WBG Annual Meeting 2018.

The scope of works include overall aspects of the event production, including room layout, branding design and production, furniture design and production, as well as AV. The IMFC seal logo was even specially produced locally in Indonesia as a 4-meter diameter round carpet.

All furnitures were made in Indonesia using local resources, and exported to IMF Office in Washington, DC, USA.
